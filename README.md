# 🛒 Freshco Hypermarket Analytics Capstone

## 🚀 Overview
This project focuses on end-to-end data analysis of an e-commerce/grocery delivery business (Freshco Hypermarket). The analysis covers order behavior, customer insights, delivery performance, and completion rates to identify key business patterns and improvement opportunities.

---

## 📊 Objective
To analyze multi-dimensional business data and generate actionable insights across:
- Orders  
- Customers  
- Deliveries  
- Completion rates  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Python (Pandas)  
- SQL  
- Excel / Data Analysis  
- Data Visualization (Power BI – optional extension)  

---

## 📌 Key Analysis Performed

### 1️⃣ Order Level Analysis
- Peak order time: **Afternoon slot**
- Highest demand area: **HSR Layout**
- Delivery charges highest during **late night**
- Maximum discounts observed in **August**

---

### 2️⃣ Completion Rate Analysis
- Average completion rate: **~99.55%**
- 100% completion observed on:
  - Sunday evenings  
  - Wednesday nights  
- Low/zero completion areas:
  - **Cox Town**
  - **Whitefield**

---

### 3️⃣ Customer Level Analysis
- Highest acquisition source: **Organic**
- Lowest performing source: **Instagram**
- Identified customers with **zero LTV**
- Revenue trends:
  - Highest: Organic  
  - Lowest: Instagram  

---

### 4️⃣ Delivery Level Analysis
- Late deliveries highest in **May**
- Faster deliveries during **late night (low traffic)**
- High delivery charges observed in:
  - **Brookfield**
  - **CV Raman Nagar**
- Weekday deliveries are faster than weekends  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Demand peaks during **afternoon due to working professionals**
- **Late-night orders** have:
  - Higher delivery charges  
  - Faster delivery time  
- **Customer acquisition quality varies by source**
- Certain areas consistently show:
  - Low completion rates  
  - High delivery delays  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 📦 Orders
- Optimize pricing & discounts based on time slots  
- Focus marketing on high-performing areas (HSR Layout)  

### 📉 Completion Rate
- Improve service in low-performing areas  
- Enhance customer trust and order reliability  

### 👥 Customers
- Reduce dependency on low-quality acquisition channels (Instagram)  
- Improve LTV through loyalty programs  

### 🚚 Delivery
- Optimize routes for high-delay areas  
- Improve weekend delivery efficiency  
- Control high delivery charges in specific locations
